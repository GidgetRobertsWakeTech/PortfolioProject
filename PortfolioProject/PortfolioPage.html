Key Principles

What am I all about?

Creativity

I strive to provide a unique, vivid angle in all the work I do. If it's not creative and visually unique, what's the point of design?

Variety

It's the spice of life! And I plan to use it liberally in every project I make. Our world shouldn't just be muted, corporate simplicity all the time.

Honesty

I want my art and projects to feel genuine, personal, and from the heart. I don't want to make my designs look like they were "phoned in", as it were.
